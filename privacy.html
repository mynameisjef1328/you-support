Privacy Policy

How You: Soft Reminders handles your information.

Last updated: December 2025

1. Information we collect

You: Soft Reminders does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

  • No account is required to use the app.
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera.

Any settings or preferences within the app are stored locally on your device and are not sent to any server that we control.

2. Analytics and third-party services

You: Soft Reminders does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Apple may collect basic, anonymized usage data as part of the normal operation of the App Store and iOS. This data is governed by Apple’s own privacy policy.

3. Data sharing

Because we do not collect your personal data, we do not sell, rent, or share it with anyone.

4. Children’s privacy

You: Soft Reminders is suitable for a general audience and does not include user accounts or social features. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to this policy

If the app’s features change in a way that affects privacy, we may update this policy. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after a policy change means you accept the updated policy.
